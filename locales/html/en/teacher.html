Evgeny Bushkov also carries out an active pedagogical activity, holds the position of head of the orchestral department string instruments of the Central Music School at the Moscow State Conservatory named after P.I. Tchaikovsky. He builds lessons around the main topics: how to approach a new essay, study as efficiently as possible and make it so that extracting sounds from the instrument was not just a performance, but the highest form of creativity.

“I am convinced that when children play music, there is a special atmosphere. We were preparing for the final concert very seriously, I taught the guys to concentrate on the depth of sound. And the young people rehearsed with great enthusiasm: they are not just performed a piece and looked for a keyword, emotion, state or association and worked with it. Energy from sincere performances - extraordinary, it will be remembered by the viewer for many years, ”says Evgeny Bushkov.

Pupils joke about the teacher: out of the millions of sounds you make, he will surely hear even one wrong one note. Now the guys hear the musical text not only from the point of view of melody and harmony, but they feel what the composer put his emotions into his composition, and they try to play as beautifully as possible.
